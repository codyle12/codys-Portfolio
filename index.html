Buster Sword

Life-sized replica inspired by Final Fantasy VII. Modeled in Fusion 360 with internal supports for structural integrity and balance.

Challenges

This was my first 3D print! There was a lot of trial and error since I was new to 3D modeling. I learned back in high-school on how to 3D model amateurishly using Fusion360, but this put my skills to the test since this was a more practical setting. I remember needing to mess with the supports and testing out several types of brims due to the parts I made constantly warping, but we got past the challenges! I played around with resin and plastic weld as a solution to fill in all the inconsistencies (and because I ran out of money for more filament) and I'm satisfied with how it came out in the end.

The final product!
Final Thoughts

I'm really happy with how the project came out! I'm really not sure why I chose to do something so huge as my first project honestly, but I admit its made doing smaller projects feel so much easier so I would gladly do it again.
